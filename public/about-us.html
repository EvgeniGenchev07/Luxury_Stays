About Us

A Few Words About Us

Nestled in the vibrant and artistic district of Kapana, our cozy apartments offer a unique experience for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the lively culture of Plovdiv. Kapana, which means "The Trap," is a creative hub known for its colorful streets, eclectic shops, buzzing cafes, and dynamic nightlife—perfect for anyone seeking to explore the true spirit of the city.

We pride ourselves on providing a home away from home. Our apartments are designed to be stylish, comfortable, and equipped with you need for a memorable stay. Whether you're visiting for a romantic getaway, a business trip, or a family vacation, our spaces offer a perfect blend of modern comfort and charming local character.

Step outside and find yourself in the heart of Plovdiv's most exciting neighborhood. From vibrant street art to cozy cafes and unique boutiques, Kapana has it all. The city's top attractions, such as the Roman Theatre and the Old Town, are just a short walk away.

Our apartments feature stylish interiors, comfortable beds, high-speed Wi-Fi, fully equipped kitchens, and smart TV. We ensure a relaxing and enjoyable stay so you can unwind after a day of exploring the city.
